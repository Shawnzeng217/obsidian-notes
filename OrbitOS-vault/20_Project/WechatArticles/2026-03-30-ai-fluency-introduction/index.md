## 一、什么是 AI 流利度 | What is AI Fluency

很多人在接触 AI 时，把精力放在工具本身：哪个模型更强、哪个 prompt 更好用、哪个平台功能最全。这条路走不远。工具在变，模型在迭代，今天的技巧明天就会过时。

*Many people approach AI by focusing on the tools themselves: which model is stronger, which prompt works best, which platform has the most features. This path doesn't go far. Tools change, models iterate, and today's tricks become obsolete tomorrow.*

**AI 流利度（AI Fluency）指的是另一件事：与 AI 系统高效、有效、合乎伦理、安全地协作的能力。**

***AI Fluency means something different: the ability to collaborate with AI systems effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely.***

这四个词不是口号，是判断你与 AI 协作质量的四把尺子：

*These four words aren't slogans. They're four measures of your AI collaboration quality:*

- **有效（Effective）**：输出是否真正达成了目标？| *Did your output truly achieve the goal?*
- **高效（Efficient）**：你用了最合适的方式，还是绕了弯路？| *Did you use the right approach, or waste effort?*
- **伦理（Ethical）**：这次协作对他人、对社会是否负责任？| *Is this collaboration responsible to others and society?*
- **安全（Safe）**：你是否评估了潜在的风险和错误？| *Did you evaluate potential risks and errors?*

四个维度缺一不可。只追求速度而忽视伦理，只关注效果而不审查错误，都不是真正的 AI 流利度。

*All four dimensions are essential. Pursuing speed while ignoring ethics, or focusing on output without checking for errors — neither qualifies as genuine AI Fluency.*

## 二、4D 核心能力框架 | The 4D Framework

Anthropic 的 AI 流利度课程提出了"4D 框架"，作为人机协作的核心能力结构。4D 对应四个英文单词，每一个都代表一种具体的协作能力。

*Anthropic's AI Fluency course introduces the "4D Framework" as the core competency structure for human-AI collaboration. Each D represents a specific collaboration capability.*

**Delegation（委派）**

知道什么任务该交给 AI，什么任务应该自己来。不是所有事情都适合 AI 处理，也不是所有事情都需要自己独立完成。委派的能力，是判断力的起点。

*Knowing which tasks to hand off to AI and which to keep yourself. Not everything suits AI, and not everything needs to be done alone. The ability to delegate is where judgment begins.*

**Description（描述）**

清楚地向 AI 表达你的意图。这不只是写 prompt，而是能把目标、背景、限制条件准确传达给 AI。描述得越清晰，协作结果越接近你的预期。

*Clearly expressing your intent to AI. This goes beyond writing prompts — it means accurately conveying your goals, context, and constraints. The clearer your description, the closer the result to your intent.*

**Discernment（辨别）**

批判性地评估 AI 的输出。AI 会犯错，会产生幻觉，会给出看起来合理但实际有问题的回答。辨别力决定你是否能识别这些问题，而不是直接接受。

*Critically evaluating AI output. AI makes mistakes, hallucinates, and produces answers that look reasonable but are actually flawed. Discernment determines whether you can identify these issues rather than accepting them outright.*

**Diligence（尽职）**

对 AI 协作的结果负责。你使用 AI 生成的内容，最终由你来承担责任。这要求你认真审查、验证、修改，而不是把 AI 的输出直接当成最终答案。

*Taking responsibility for the results of AI collaboration. You are ultimately accountable for the AI-generated content you use. This requires careful review, verification, and revision — not treating AI output as a final answer.*

这四个维度不是孤立的技能，而是一套完整的协作思维框架。掌握其中一个不等于掌握全部，它们相互依存。

*These four dimensions are not isolated skills — they form a complete collaboration thinking framework. Mastering one doesn't mean mastering all; they are interdependent.*

## 三、为什么需要一套持久的框架 | Why a Lasting Framework Matters

AI 工具的生命周期越来越短。一个工具从流行到被替代，有时只需要几个月。如果你的 AI 能力建立在某个具体工具或某套固定 prompt 上，这套能力会随工具一起过时。

*AI tools have increasingly short lifecycles. A tool can go from popular to obsolete in months. If your AI capabilities are built on specific tools or fixed prompts, those capabilities become obsolete with them.*

框架不同。框架关注的是人与 AI 协作的底层逻辑——无论对方是 Claude、GPT、Gemini，还是三年后出现的新模型，这套逻辑都适用。技术在变，协作的核心问题不变：你想让 AI 做什么，你如何传达，你如何判断结果，你如何承担责任。

*A framework is different. It focuses on the underlying logic of human-AI collaboration — applicable whether you're working with Claude, GPT, Gemini, or whatever model appears three years from now. Technology changes, but the core questions of collaboration don't: what you want AI to do, how you communicate it, how you judge the result, how you take responsibility.*

这门课程的目标不是教你用某个工具，而是帮你建立一套会随着你一起成长的协作能力。

*The goal of this course isn't to teach you a specific tool — it's to help you build a set of collaboration capabilities that grow with you.*

## 四、如何开始实践 | How to Start Practicing

课程的每个模块都配有练习，要求直接与语言模型对话。工具没有限制：Claude（claude.ai 免费注册）、ChatGPT 或其他你熟悉的 AI 都可以。核心原则在不同 AI 系统之间通用。

*Each module includes exercises requiring direct conversation with a language model. No specific tool required: Claude (free at claude.ai), ChatGPT, or any AI you're comfortable with. The core principles apply across different AI systems.*

开始之前，试着回答这三个问题——不需要标准答案，它们帮你建立自己的学习坐标：

*Before starting, try answering these three questions — no right answers required. They help you establish your own learning reference point:*

1. 你目前在用 AI 时，遇到过哪些让你失望的结果？
   *What outcomes have disappointed you when using AI so far?*

2. 你最期待 AI 在哪些场景下帮助你？
   *In which scenarios are you most excited about AI helping you?*

3. 你希望学完这个系列后，改变什么？
   *What do you hope to change after completing this series?*

## 重点总结 | Key Takeaways

**AI 流利度不是工具技能，是协作能力。**
***AI Fluency is not a tool skill — it's a collaboration capability.***

| 核心概念 | 说明 | Core Concept | Explanation |
|---------|------|-------------|-------------|
| AI Fluency | 与AI有效、高效、伦理、安全地协作 | AI Fluency | Collaborating with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, safely |
| Delegation | 判断哪些任务交给AI | Delegation | Deciding which tasks to hand off to AI |
| Description | 清晰传达意图给AI | Description | Clearly expressing intent to AI |
| Discernment | 批判性评估AI输出 | Discernment | Critically evaluating AI output |
| Diligence | 对AI协作结果负责 | Diligence | Taking responsibility for AI collaboration results |

**三个关键认知：**

1. 框架比工具更持久——AI 迭代不会让框架过时
2. 与 AI 协作需要思维方式的转变，不只是技能习得
3. 你对 AI 输出负有最终责任，辨别和尽职不可省略

***Three key insights:***

1. *Frameworks outlast tools — AI iteration won't make a framework obsolete*
2. *Collaborating with AI requires a mindset shift, not just skill acquisition*
3. *You bear final responsibility for AI output; discernment and diligence cannot be skipped*
